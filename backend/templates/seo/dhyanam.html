Geeta Dhyanam

Nine sacred invocation verses traditionally recited before studying the Bhagavad Geeta

Introduction

The Geeta Dhyanam consists of nine shlokas from the Geeta Mahatmya (found in the Padma Purana). These verses are traditionally recited as an invocation before beginning study of the Bhagavad Geeta, offering salutations to the scripture, to Sage Vyasa, and to Lord Krishna.
